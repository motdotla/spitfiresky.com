About Spitfire Sky

We are a web software company. We build websites and web applications using technologies like Ruby on Rails, Merb, and Wordpress.

The company is mostly just me (Scott Motte), but I occasionally work alongside other freelancers to get the job done. I do good work at reasonable rates, and I am honest and diligent.

We are also the team behind Twinstang which provides online digital logbooking for pilots.

To find out more about me, visit my blog or email me at scott@spitfiresky.com.
