	<h2>About Spitfire Sky</h2>

	<p>
		We are a web software company. <strong>We build websites and web applications using technologies like <a href="http://rubyonrails.org/">Ruby on Rails</a>, <a href="http://merbivore.com/">Merb</a>, and <a href="http://wordpress.org">Wordpress</a>.</strong>
	</p>
	
	<p>
		<a href="http://scottmotte.com"><img src="/images/scott.jpg" class="rthumb"></a>
		The company is mostly just me (Scott Motte), but I occasionally work alongside other freelancers to get the job done. <em>I do good work at reasonable rates, and I am honest and diligent.</em></p>
	
	<p>We are also the team behind <a href="http://twinstang.com">Twinstang</a> which provides online digital logbooking for pilots.</p>
